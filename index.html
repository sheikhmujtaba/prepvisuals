What makes a good visual?

A Good Visual

The hockey stick chart is portraying information with more clarity, as you can easily conclude the data from this chart quite easily. In addition, this chart is less misleading comparative to other charts, as it conveys data with honesty. Moreover, the chart is aesthetically more pleasing than other charts, as it gives only the required information and does not confuse the viewer with a lot of information. The steepness of the slope and the movement of the graph depicts the trend in a manner which can be understood by a layman. Moreover, this visual functional in the sense that anybody can look at it and extract information from it, and make conclusion on the data that is represented by the figure. This visual shows a good relationship between two variables and makes a good comparsion on how a change in our independent variable, causes a change in the dependent variable.
